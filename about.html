ABOUT

I am Liam Wiggins, a dedicated and experienced personal trainer who is passionate about helping individuals achieve their fitness aspirations. I hold a degree in Sports and Exercise Science from the University of Central Lancaster, where I gained comprehensive knowledge of human physiology, biomechanics, and exercise principles. This educational foundation has been instrumental in shaping my approach to training and guiding clients towards optimal performance and well-being.

As a fully qualified personal trainer and physiotherapist, I have honed my skills to not only design effective workout programs but also provide valuable insights into injury prevention and rehabilitation. My dual expertise allows me to create tailored training regimens that prioritize both physical progress and long-term health. I firmly believe that a holistic approach to fitness is essential for sustainable results.

Beyond my professional pursuits, I have a deep appreciation for an active lifestyle. You can often find me outdoors, taking my beloved dog for invigorating walks that offer a refreshing break from training routines. Rugby has been a longstanding passion of mine, reflecting my commitment to teamwork, discipline, and pushing boundaries. Equally important, I cherish moments spent with friends and family, recognizing the significance of balance in maintaining a fulfilling life.

My training philosophy centers on fostering a positive and motivating environment. I understand that each individual's journey is unique, and I am dedicated to tailoring my guidance to align with personal goals and preferences. Whether you're aiming to enhance your athletic performance, recover from an injury, or simply lead a healthier lifestyle, I am committed to supporting you every step of the way. By working together, we will not only achieve your fitness goals but also cultivate a sustainable and enjoyable approach to health that extends far beyond the gym.
